About

Hi, my name is Brian, and I am a Canadian based in London. I came to the UK to study, interested in how the state can utilise technology to benefit the lives of citizens. I was fortunate enough to put my academic interests into practice through working on a diverse range of digital transformation projects across government.

I am passionate about advocating for the responsible use of technology and learning how user centred design can improve the delivery of government services. Currently enjoying my job as a Service Designer at the Ministry of Justice, I created this personal website so you can learn more about me.
